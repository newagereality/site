BIBLE Prophecies • 6 Min Read

The Book of Isaiah

Isaiah 60

6 Herds of camels will cover your land, young camels of Midian and Ephah. And all from Sheba will come, bearing gold and incense and proclaiming the praise of the Lord.

7 All the flocks of Kedar shall be gathered together to you, The rams of Nebaioth shall minister to you; They shall ascend with acceptance on My altar, And I will glorify the house of My glory.

Isaiah 60:6-7

  • According to Bible Central: "The Midianites were Arabians dwelling principally in the desert north of the peninsula of Arabia. Their progenitor was Midian, son of Abraham..."

  • According to the McClintock and Strong Biblical Cyclopedia : "Sheba seems to have been the name of the great South Arabian kingdom and the peoples which composed it..."

These are the names of the sons of Ishmael... Nebaioth the firstborn of Ishmael, Kedar, Adbeel, Mibsam

Genesis 25:13

  • According to the McClintock and Strong Biblical Cyclopedia : "[Nebai'oth] 1. The first-born son of Ishmael (Ge 25:13; 1Ch 1:29), and the prince or sheik... of one of the twelve Ishmaelitish tribes... 2. A tribe of Ishmaelites, descendants of the above... first settled in the wilderness "before" (i.e, to the east of) the other descendants of Abraham; i.e., in the great desert lying to the east and south-east of Palestine."

The glorified house of the Arabs, the sons of Abraham, which they all travel to for worship, is the Kaabah. The passage is saying that these people are true worshippers of the Lord of Isaiah.
